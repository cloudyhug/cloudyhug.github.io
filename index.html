Profile

As a student in 5th year at INSA Rennes, I have acquired various skills in computer science thanks to all the courses I have followed, in the core curriculum (compilers, systems & networks, software and hardware architecture, ...), within the Large Scale Systems specialisation curriculum (performance evaluation, parallel & distributed computing), as well as in the different optional courses (AI, mobile programming).

My favourite fields are compilers, high performance computing, and systems & networks programming. I have discovered numerous languages, through various programming paradigms. The languages I have been able to apply during internships and which I best handle are OCaml and Java, but I also have a good level in C/C++. Besides, I have occasionally used Ruby, Python, Haskell, and Clojure.

I have always enjoyed basic science and theoretical computer science, that is why I am following courses for a research master's degree in 2019/2020. I will have the opportunity to improve my knowledge and my skills regarding compilers, adding a security and low level flavour to my technical background.
